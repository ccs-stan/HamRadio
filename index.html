Baofeng Radio Keys

This is a quick summary of the key usage for the Baofeng radio.

# (press and hold)Allows you to scan through TX/RX frequencies
* (press and hold)Lock/Unlock keys
0 (press and hold)Battery Voltage
A/BSelect VHF/UHF frequency
#/^ (up)Select High/low output power
ExitAllows you to exit the menu you have selected or are currently viewing
StepAllows you to access your frequency steps (2.5/5/6.25/10/12.5/20/25/50kHz)
TXPAllows you to switch and set transmission power (High/Medium/Low)
SaveAllows you to access your Battery Saver settings (OFF/1/2/3/4)
VOXAllows you to set your voice operated transmission settings (OFF/0 - 10)
W NAllows you to switch between Wideband and Narrowband settings (WIDE/NARR)
ABRAllows you to adjust the display illumination time length. (Off/1/2/3/4/5 seconds)
SQLAllows you to access the squelch settings and adjust squelch levels (0 - 9)
TDRAllows you to switch between dual watch and dual reception. (Off/On)
BEEPAllows you to adjust the keypad's beep sound. (Off/On
TOTAllows you to access your transmission timer settings. (15/30/45/60.../585/600 seconds
